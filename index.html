Sewing

Sewing is the craft of fastening or attaching objects using stitches made with a sewing needle and thread. Sewing is one of the oldest of the textile arts, arising in the Paleolithic era.

"Beautiful things come together one stitch at a time" - Anonymous

Here are some facts about sewing:

  • Cotton lasts for 100 years
  • Sewing was one of the first skills humans learnt
  • More than 4000 types of sewing machines have been invented, only the simplest ones lasted as the others had many problems.
